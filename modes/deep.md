# Modo: deep — Deep Research Prompt

Generates a structured research prompt for Perplexity/Claude/ChatGPT with 6 PMM-focused axes:

```
## Deep Research: [Company] — [Role]

Context: I'm evaluating a PMM candidacy at [company] for [role]. I need actionable intelligence for interviews and to calibrate my positioning.

### 1. ICP & Customer Segmentation
- Who are their primary buyers? (title, function, company size, industry)
- Who are the end users vs. the economic buyers?
- What are the top 3 pain points they sell against?
- Any public case studies or customer stories that reveal the ICP?

### 2. GTM Motion & Marketing Strategy
- Sales-led, PLG, or hybrid? What does the top-of-funnel look like?
- How do they generate pipeline? (inbound, outbound, partner, community)
- What's their pricing/packaging model? (per seat, usage-based, platform tiers)
- How does PMM sit in the org — under Marketing, Product, or a separate function?
- Recent campaigns, product launches, or repositioning signals?

### 3. Competitive Positioning
- Who are their main competitors? How do they differentiate?
- What's their stated moat or category claim?
- Are they building a new category or competing in an existing one?
- Any win/loss intel from review sites (G2, TrustRadius, Gartner Peer Insights)?

### 4. PMM Team & Culture Signals
- How large is the PMM team? Any recent PMM hires on LinkedIn?
- Any leadership changes in Marketing or Product in the last 6 months?
- Do they publish PMM thinking externally? (blog, talks, case studies)
- Glassdoor/Blind signals on marketing/PMM culture

### 5. Recent Moves (last 6 months)
- Funding rounds, M&A, or partnerships?
- Major product launches or feature announcements?
- Expansion into new markets or verticals?
- Exec hires or departures (CEO, CMO, CPO)?

### 6. Candidate Angle
Given my profile (read from cv.md and profile.yml for specific experience):
- What positioning problem does this company likely have that I've solved before?
- Which of my proof points maps most directly to their GTM motion?
- What's the APAC/India angle I can bring to this company's expansion story?
- What would I launch or reposition in my first 90 days?
```

Personalize each section with the specific context of the evaluated offer.
